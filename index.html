Introduction#

pysradb provides a simple method to programmatically access metadata and download sequencing data from NCBI’s Sequence Read Archive (SRA) and European Bioinformatics Institute’s European Nucleotide Archive (ENA).

Quick Example#

To fetch metadata associated with project accession SRP265425

$ pysradb metadata SRP265425
